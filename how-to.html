How It Works

A simple guide to turning your pain into laughter.

1 Sign Up (or Don't)

Getting started is easy. You can sign up with an email or Google account. Want to stay completely private? Just flip the Anonymous Mode switch. No one will know who you are.

2 Create a Joke

This is where the magic happens. Tell the AI what's bothering you. Add a few keywords or a short sentence. You can even give it hints, like "make it punny" or "keep it clean," and choose a style like "Self-Deprecating." The AI will turn your pain into a one-liner.

3 Track Your Progress

Your private Pain Diary keeps a record of the pains you've entered. The calendar view shows you which days you've used the app, helping you see patterns and track your journey over time. This is for your eyes only.

4 Join the Community

Browse feeds of jokes from others, see what's trending, and save your favorites. If you feel like sharing, you can contribute your own jokes to the community feed. Your support on our social channels also helps us grow and keep the app free!
